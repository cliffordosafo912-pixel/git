Fish Habitat

Many fish live in the ocean including sharks, tuna, and salmon.

Freshwater fish like tilapia and catfish live in rivers and lakes.

Clownfish and other tropical fish live in coral reef ecosystems.

Food & Diet

Small fish feed on plankton and microorganisms.

Predatory fish like sharks eat smaller fish.

Some fish feed on plants and algae for nutrients.
